Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 2, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Pathway Parking ("we", "our", or "the app") handles information when you use the game.

Information We Collect

We do not require you to create an account, and we do not directly collect your name, email address, phone number, or other contact information.

The app may store gameplay-related information locally on your device, such as game progress, unlocked levels, settings, hints balance, and purchase state.

Advertising

The app uses Google AdMob to show ads. AdMob may collect and use information such as device identifiers, IP address, approximate location, app interactions, diagnostic information, and advertising data in order to provide, limit, measure, and improve ads.

Google may use this information in accordance with its own policies. You can learn more about how Google uses data here: https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites

In-App Purchases

The app offers in-app purchases, such as removing ads and purchasing hints. Purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive or store your payment card details.

We may store the purchase state locally on your device so the app can remember whether ads have been removed or how many hints are available.

Third-Party Services

The app may use the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob for advertising
  • Apple App Store / StoreKit for in-app purchases

These services may process information according to their own privacy policies.

Children's Privacy

The app is not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe that a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. Data may be processed by third-party services listed above for advertising, purchase processing, analytics, fraud prevention, and service operation.

Data Retention

Gameplay progress and settings may remain on your device until you delete the app or clear its data. Data processed by third-party services is retained according to their own policies.

Your Choices

You can disable personalized advertising where supported by your device settings. You can also remove ads by purchasing the no-ads option in the app.

Changes to This Policy
